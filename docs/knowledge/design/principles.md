# Design Principles

This site is Kuba's public technical lab, not a polished personal-brand portfolio.

Optimize for:

- curiosity and technical exploration
- useful writing and build notes
- visible process over perfect polish
- direct, personal copy
- brutalist, engineered structure

Do not optimize primarily for:

- conversion
- resume polish
- generic portfolio aesthetics
- SaaS-style marketing smoothness

## Design implications

- The homepage should feel like an index/logbook, not a marketing landing page.
- Posts, projects, and pages may expose process, failures, experiments, and changing opinions.
- Projects should feel like lab artifacts or case files rather than polished portfolio cards.
- Rough edges are acceptable when they feel intentional and usable; broken or inaccessible states are not.
- The existing brutalist dark visual language is a good fit and should remain the baseline.
